Our History

TechCon started in 2010 as a small meetup of developers in Silicon Valley. Over the years, it has grown into one of the largest global conferences for tech professionals, innovators, and learners.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to inspire, educate, and connect the next generation of tech leaders. We focus on innovation, inclusiveness, and global impact in the tech community.
